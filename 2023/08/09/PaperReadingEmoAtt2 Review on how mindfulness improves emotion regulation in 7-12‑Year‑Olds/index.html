PaperReadingEmoAtt2 Review on how mindfulness improves emotion regulation in 7-12‑Year‑Olds

Posted on 2023-08-09

one sentence summary

The review explored the impact of mindfulness-based interventions (MBIs) or cognitive behavioral interventions (CBIs) on emotion regulation in 7 to 12 years old children, and it found that MBIs significantly improve the emotion awareness, rise the positive emotion and reduce depression while CBIs ameliorate the negative emotion expression, and universal interventions with a shorter duration had more significant effects in MBIs researches, particularly for 10–12-year-old participants, which indicates the importance of considering age as a mediator in future studies of this field.

method

Search for published and unpublished randomised controlled trials in databases (British Education Index, Child Development and Adolescent Studies, CINAHL, Education Source, ERIC, MEDLINE, PsycINFO, PubMed, SAGE, Web of Science) , which evaluating the effect of primary school-based MBIs (n = 18) or CBIs (n= 12) on measures of emotional awareness, modulation and expression.

一句话总结

这篇综述探讨了正念干预（MBIs）和认知行为干预（CBIs）对7-12岁儿童情绪调节的影响，发现MBIs显著提高情绪觉知，增加积极情绪，减少抑郁情绪，而CBIs改善负面情绪表达习惯，另外，持续时间较短的普遍干预对MBIs研究有更显著的影响，特别是对于10-12岁的学生来说，这可能提示着该领域中的未来研究需要更多考虑年龄，针对小学儿童设计合适的干预方案。
